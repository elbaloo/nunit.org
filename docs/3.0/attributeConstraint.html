Attribute Constraints (NUnit 3.0)

Attribute constraints are used to test for the existence of attributes on classes, methods, properties, etc.

AttributeConstraint & AttributeExistsConstraint

Action

Tests for the existence of an attribute. The differences between the two constraints are in their position in the syntax.

Constructor

AttributeConstraint(Type type, IConstraint baseConstraint)
AttributeExistsConstraint(Type type)

Syntax

Has.Attribute( typeof(TestFixtureAttribute) )
Has.Attribute<TestFixtureAttribute>()

Examples of Use

Assert.That( someObject, Has.Attribute( typeof(TestFixtureAttribute) ));
Assert.That( someObject, Has.Attribute<TestFixtureAttribute>() ));
